How Edge Works

Positive edge: Our model thinks the home team is MORE likely to win than Vegas implies. If we say 70% and Vegas implies 60%, that's a +10% edge on the home side.

Negative edge: Our model thinks the home team is LESS likely than Vegas implies. A -10% edge means we'd bet the away team.

Value threshold: Edges >5% are flagged as potential value bets. Edges >10% are strong disagreements worth watching.

This is not betting advice. Edge calculation shows where our model disagrees with the market, which can indicate model error just as easily as market inefficiency.
