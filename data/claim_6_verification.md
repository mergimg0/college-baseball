# Claim #6: College baseball betting handle "$150-450M"

**Source:** v0v1_unified_specification.md:52 ("Orange Team estimate")
**Date verified:** 2026-04-09

## Verdict: UNVERIFIABLE (no authoritative source found)

## Evidence Searched

- American Gaming Association (AGA): Publishes annual "Commercial Gaming Revenue Tracker"
  but does NOT break out college baseball as a separate category. Reports total sports
  betting handle by state.
- Nevada Gaming Control Board: Reports monthly handle by sport category but groups
  "baseball" (MLB + college) together. No college-specific breakout.
- UNLV Center for Gaming Research: Academic papers on sports betting economics but
  no college baseball specific data found.
- Legal Sports Report: Covers state-level handle data but no college baseball breakout.

## Assessment

The "$150-450M" estimate appears to be an extrapolation:
- Total US sports betting handle 2025: ~$120B
- College baseball is a fraction of college sports (~2-3% of college betting)
- College sports betting ~$10-15B → college baseball ~$200-450M

This range is plausible but unverified. No authoritative public data source
provides a college baseball handle number.

## Recommendation

Use "college baseball betting is a growing but niche market" rather than
citing specific dollar figures without sources.
